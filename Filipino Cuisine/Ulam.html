Ulam (Main Dishes)

Explore a variety of traditional Filipino main dishes that are sure to satisfy your hunger and bring flavor to your table. These savory meals are commonly served with rice and are perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Chicken Adobo

A savory and tangy dish made with chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, simmered to perfection.

Sinigang na Baboy

A sour pork soup made with tamarind, tomatoes, and vegetables, offering a comforting and tangy flavor.

Kare-Kare

A rich stew made with oxtail, vegetables, and peanut sauce, traditionally served with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) on the side.

Pancit Canton

A savory stir-fried noodle dish with vegetables, pork, chicken, and sometimes seafood, flavored with soy sauce and garlic.

Lumpiang Shanghai

Filipino spring rolls filled with seasoned ground pork and vegetables, deep-fried to crispy perfection.
