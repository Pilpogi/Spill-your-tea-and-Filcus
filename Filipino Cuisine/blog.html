The Ultimate Guide to Making Chicken Adobo

Posted on: November 4, 2024 | By: Annie Ilagan

When it comes to Filipino cuisine, few dishes are as iconic as Chicken Adobo. This savory, tangy dish is a staple in many Filipino households and is known for its bold flavors and ease of preparation...

What is Chicken Adobo?

Adobo is a traditional Filipino dish that involves marinating meat (usually chicken or pork) in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns...

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into pieces, bone-in preferred)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar (cane vinegar is ideal)
  • 1 head of garlic (minced)
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 2-3 potatoes (sliced, for added texture)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces, soy sauce, minced garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns...
  2. Sauté: In a large pot or pan, heat the cooking oil over medium heat...
  3. Add Marinade and Water: Once the chicken is browned, pour in the reserved marinade and add 1 cup of water...
  4. Simmer: Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes...
  5. Adjust Seasoning: Taste the sauce and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed...
  6. Serve: Serve hot over steamed white rice...

Tips and Tricks

Feel free to experiment with the ingredients. Some people like to add coconut milk for creaminess, or sugar for a hint of sweetness...

Conclusion

Chicken Adobo is more than just a dish; it’s a celebration of Filipino culture and family. I hope this guide inspires you to try making your own adobo at home...
