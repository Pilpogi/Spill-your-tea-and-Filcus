Merienda (Snacks)

Explore the delightful Filipino merienda (snacks) that are perfect for any time of the day! From crispy, sweet treats to refreshing desserts, these snacks are enjoyed by Filipinos everywhere.

Turon

A snack made from ripe bananas wrapped in a spring roll wrapper, fried, and coated in caramelized sugar.

Bibingka

A rice cake made with rice flour and coconut milk, topped with salted egg and cheese.

Halo-Halo

Leche Flan

A rich and creamy Filipino dessert made with eggs, sweetened condensed milk, and caramel. It's a favorite at celebrations and is known for its smooth texture and sweet flavor.
