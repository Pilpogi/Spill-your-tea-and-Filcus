Bibingka

Bibingka is a traditional Filipino rice cake made with rice flour and coconut milk, usually topped with salted egg and cheese. It's often enjoyed during the Christmas season.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 salted eggs, sliced
  • Grated cheese (preferably queso de bola)
  • Banana leaves (for lining the baking dish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the Baking Dish: Line a round baking dish with banana leaves, making sure to cover the bottom and sides.
  3. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, combine rice flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Combine Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, mix coconut milk, water, and melted butter. Add the wet mixture to the dry ingredients and stir until well combined.
  5. Pour the Batter: Pour half of the batter into the prepared baking dish. Arrange slices of salted eggs and sprinkle with grated cheese on top.
  6. Add Remaining Batter: Pour the remaining batter over the top and add more salted egg and cheese on top if desired.
  7. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool and Serve: Allow to cool slightly before cutting into slices. Serve warm, and enjoy your Bibingka!

Tips

  • You can add toppings like grated coconut or more cheese for extra flavor.
  • To enhance the flavor, you can use fresh coconut milk if available.
  • Adjust the sugar level according to your preference!
