Leche Flan

Leche Flan is a rich and creamy Filipino dessert made with eggs, sweetened condensed milk, and caramel. It's a favorite at celebrations and is known for its smooth texture and sweet flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar (for caramel)
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: zest of 1 lemon or lime for flavor

Instructions

  1. Make the Caramel: In a saucepan over low heat, melt the sugar until it turns golden brown. Quickly pour the caramel into a round llanera (flan mold) or any heatproof dish, tilting to coat the bottom evenly. Set aside to cool.
  2. Prepare the Mixture: In a mixing bowl, combine sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, egg yolks, and vanilla extract. If using, add the zest for extra flavor. Whisk until smooth.
  3. Strain: Strain the mixture through a fine sieve to remove any lumps. This ensures a smooth texture for your flan.
  4. Pour and Steam: Pour the mixture over the cooled caramel in the llanera. Cover with aluminum foil. Steam over low heat for about 30-40 minutes, or until set. You can use a steamer or a pot with a steaming rack.
  5. Cool and Serve: Allow to cool completely at room temperature, then refrigerate for a few hours. To serve, run a knife around the edges and invert onto a serving plate. Enjoy your Leche Flan!

Tips

  • Ensure the caramel is not burnt, as it can affect the flavor.
  • You can bake the flan in a water bath if you prefer that method.
  • Leche Flan can be made a day in advance for better flavor.
